Specialist Advantage

Why a Biting Pest Specialist Gets Better Results

Species-Level Identification

General pest companies treat symptoms. We identify the exact species and target its biology — different baits for carpenter ants vs pavement ants, heat vs chemical for bed bugs.

Bite-Specific Treatment Protocols

Every biting pest has a different life cycle, harbourage pattern, and resistance profile. Our protocols are built for these 6 pests — not adapted from a general playbook.

Written Pest-Specific Guarantees

90 days for bed bugs. 60 days for fleas and ticks. 30 days for ants and spiders. Season-long for wasps. Every guarantee is written and specific — not a vague promise.

Health Risk Guidance

We help you understand the health implications of each bite — when it is harmless, when to see a doctor, and how to prevent secondary infections.

Pest-Specific Guarantees

  • Fleas & ticks: 60-day free return
