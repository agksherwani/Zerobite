Specialist Advantage

Why a Biting Pest Specialist Gets Better Results

Species-Level Identification

Generalists spray and hope. ZeroBite technicians identify the exact species under magnification, then deploy the precise bait, barrier, or heat protocol engineered for that organism. Carpenter ant vs pavement ant. Tropical bed bug vs common bed bug. The distinction determines the cure.

Bite-Specific Treatment Protocols

Each of our 6 protocols was built from scratch for a single pest — not borrowed from a general playbook. Different life cycles demand different timing. Different harbourage patterns demand different access strategies. We treat the biology, not the symptom.

Written Pest-Specific Guarantees

Bed bugs: 90-day written guarantee. Fleas and ticks: 60 days. Ants and spiders: 30 days. Wasps: season-long nest-free pledge. Every guarantee names the pest, the treatment, and the exact terms. No fine print. No grey area.

Health Risk Guidance

Biting pests carry real medical consequences — from Lyme disease (ticks) to anaphylaxis (wasps) to secondary bacterial infections (bed bugs, fleas). Our technicians brief you on the clinical risks specific to your infestation and advise when a physician visit is warranted.
